Help Ukraine!


Ukraine needs your help! The Government of Ukraine has set up an Ethereum address to facilitate cryptocurrency donations. You can check it here.
If you have a browser wallet like Metamask, you can send ether by following these steps:

Step 1.

Click on the "Connect" button to use your browser wallet.



Step 2.

Choose the amount you want to donate (the dollar amount will be automatically converted to ether).
You can directly enter the amount in the textbox "Enter amount in dollars" (e.g. 15.25).
Then click the red button ("Donate a different amount") to complete the donation.


You can check the code here :)
